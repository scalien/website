Company

Background

Formed in 2009, Scalien is a NoSQL database company co-founded by Marton Trencseni, Attila Gazso and Fred Eberlein, with backgrounds in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Physics. Formerly, Marton and Attila worked as software engineers while Fred has a background in startup management and business development. Scalien is based in Boston and Budapest, the latter serving as the development hub.

Mission Statement

Scalien technology enables companies to build the most reliable large-scale web services.

About

Cloud computing is resulting in unprecedented data management workloads that are beyond the reach of traditional SQL databases. While SQL databases remain at the heart of corporate information systems, NoSQL has emerged to address these new challenges. ScalienDB unites the strengths of SQL databases with the strengths of NoSQL databases. ScalienDB was designed from the ground up to be a highly available and scalable transactional NoSQL database. Built with the Paxos algorithm at its core, Scalien enables businesses to build the most reliable and scalable software services. Customers range from venture-backed startups to publicly traded companies using ScalienDB to power their cloud applications.
