Company and Mission

About Scalien

Founded in 2009, Scalien is a NoSQL database company co-founded by Marton Trencseni, Attila Gazso and Fred Eberlein, with backgrounds in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Physics. Formerly, Marton and Attila worked as software engineers while Fred was CEO of ReliefInsite, later acquired by PatientsLikeMe. Backed by investments from LogMeIn, Scalien is based in Boston and Budapest, the latter serving as our main development hub.

Mission Statement

Cloud computing is resulting in unprecedented data management workloads that are beyond the reach of traditional SQL databases, like MySQL and Oracle. While SQL databases remain at the heart of corporate information systems, NoSQL has emerged to address these new challenges.

ScalienDB unites the strengths of SQL databases with the strengths of NoSQL databases. ScalienDB was designed from the ground up to be a highly available and scalable transactional NoSQL database. Scalien enables businesses to build the most reliable and scalable software services. We focuses on strong engineering principles like the use of the Paxos algorithm and strong co-operation with our enterprise customers and partners.
